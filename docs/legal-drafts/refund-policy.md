# Boss of Clean — Lead Fee Refund Policy

**Applies to:** the $30 lead capture fee paid by service professionals
("Pros") on Boss of Clean (bossofclean.com), a Florida home and business
services marketplace operated under Florida law.

---

## 1. What the lead fee buys

A Pro pays the lead fee only after a customer has (a) accepted the Pro's
quote and (b) confirmed they want to hire that Pro. Payment releases the
customer's contact information (name, phone, email, service address) to that
Pro for that job. The fee is for the introduction — it is not a guarantee
that the job will be completed or that the customer will not change plans.

## 2. Bad-lead criteria (refund-eligible)

A lead fee is refundable when the lead was defective at the time of
purchase, meaning any of:

1. **Disconnected or wrong number / wrong contact info.** The phone number is
   disconnected, unreachable as dialed, or belongs to someone other than the
   requester; or the email hard-bounces.
2. **Fake or fraudulent request.** The request was not submitted by a real
   prospective customer (spam, pranks, bots, competitor probing).
3. **Duplicate lead.** The Pro already paid for the same customer + same job
   on Boss of Clean (a repeat request for a *new* job is not a duplicate).
4. **Customer cancelled before contact.** The customer withdrew the request
   before the Pro had any reasonable opportunity to make contact.
5. **Outside stated service area.** The job address is materially outside the
   service area the lead displayed before purchase.

## 3. Not refund-eligible

- The customer chose another Pro, postponed, or negotiated a different scope.
- The Pro was slow to respond (we recommend contact within 24 hours).
- Price disagreement after contact.
- The Pro simply changed their mind about wanting the job.

## 4. Claim window

Refund claims must be submitted within **7 calendar days** of the fee being
charged. Claims outside the window are reviewed only at Boss of Clean's
discretion.

## 5. How to claim and resolution SLA

1. Submit the refund request from the Pro dashboard (or email
   admin@bossofclean.com) with the lead reference and the reason category.
2. We may verify the claim (e.g., test the phone number, review platform
   messages and request history). One claim per lead.
3. **SLA:** decision within **5 business days** of submission. Approved
   refunds are returned to the original payment method within 10 business
   days (card network timing may add processing days).

## 6. Form of refund

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Where the Pro
prefers, we may offer an account credit toward a future lead instead.

## 7. Abuse

Refund claims are audited. Submitting false claims, or claiming a refund on
a lead the Pro in fact serviced (on or off platform), is grounds for claim
denial, clawback, and account suspension. See also the Anti-Circumvention
Policy.
