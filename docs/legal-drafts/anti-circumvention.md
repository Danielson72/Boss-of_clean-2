# Boss of Clean — Anti-Circumvention Policy

> **DRAFT — FOR ATTORNEY REVIEW (DLD-276).** This document is a working draft
> prepared to speed up counsel review. It is not legal advice and is not in
> force until approved and published.

**Purpose:** Boss of Clean's model is simple — Pros pay a small, flat fee
only when a customer confirms a hire. That only works if introductions made
on the platform are honored on the platform.

---

## 1. The rule

If a Pro and a customer are introduced through Boss of Clean, the resulting
job (and its acceptance flow) must run through Boss of Clean. Specifically,
for **12 months** after the introduction, a Pro may not:

- solicit or accept the introduced customer's job **off-platform to avoid
  the lead fee** (e.g., "cancel the request and just call me");
- share personal contact info in pre-payment chat, or use the platform's
  messaging to move the transaction off-platform before paying the fee;
- misrepresent a serviced lead as "bad" to claim a refund (see Refund
  Policy §7).

Customers likewise agree not to help a Pro bypass the fee after being
introduced on the platform (e.g., resubmitting the same job directly to the
same Pro off-platform at the Pro's request).

## 2. What is NOT circumvention

- A pre-existing relationship: the Pro can show they serviced this customer
  **before** the platform introduction (invoices, message history).
- Jobs after the 12-month window.
- A customer independently finding the Pro through channels unrelated to
  Boss of Clean (counsel: consider burden-of-proof wording).
- Normal off-platform communication **after** the Pro has paid the lead fee
  for that job — post-payment, the relationship is theirs.

## 3. Enforcement ladder

1. **First verified violation:** written warning + payment of the avoided
   lead fee.
2. **Second:** 30-day suspension of the Pro account + payment of avoided
   fees.
3. **Third or egregious (organized bypass, refund fraud):** permanent
   removal. Boss of Clean may also pursue recovery of avoided fees.

Platform signals used in verification may include pre-payment message
content (the platform blocks contact-info exchange pre-payment by design),
cancellation-then-completion patterns, and customer reports.

## 4. Liquidated fee recovery (counsel to validate)

For each verified circumvented job, the Pro owes the standard lead fee that
would have applied. *Counsel note:* confirm enforceability of this as
liquidated damages (reasonable, not punitive) under Florida law, and whether
it should live in the Pro Terms of Service with an acknowledgment at
signup.*

## 5. Reporting

Customers or Pros can report suspected circumvention to
admin@bossofclean.com. Reports are confidential; retaliation against a
reporter is itself a violation.

## 6. Florida notes for counsel

- This policy is a platform-usage term, not a non-compete; confirm framing
  so it is not construed under Fla. Stat. § 542.335 (restrictive covenants).
- Confirm the 12-month window and the enforcement ladder are proportionate
  and consistently applied (FDUTPA fairness optics).
- Coordinate with the Terms of Service venue/arbitration clauses.
