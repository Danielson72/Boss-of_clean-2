# Boss of Clean — Review Policy

> **DRAFT — FOR ATTORNEY REVIEW (DLD-276).** This document is a working draft
> prepared to speed up counsel review. It is not legal advice and is not in
> force until approved and published.

**Purpose:** reviews on Boss of Clean must reflect real, verified service
experiences. "Purrfection is our Standard" only means something if the
ratings are honest.

---

## 1. Who can review

Only a customer who **booked and received (or materially began) a service
through Boss of Clean** may review the Pro for that job. One review per
completed job. Pros may post a public response to any review of their
business.

## 2. Verification requirement

Reviews are tied to a platform booking record. We do not accept:
- reviews from accounts with no booking with that Pro;
- reviews of a Pro by that Pro, their employees, or family/associates;
- imported or copied reviews from other sites.

Reviews may display a "Verified job" indicator only when the underlying
booking exists on the platform.

## 3. No pay-for-reviews

Neither Boss of Clean nor Pros may:
- pay, discount, tip, or otherwise compensate anyone for a positive review;
- condition a refund or dispute resolution on editing/removing a review;
- run "review gating" (soliciting reviews only from customers screened as
  happy).

Asking a customer, neutrally, to leave an honest review is allowed and
encouraged.

*Counsel note:* align this section with the federal Consumer Review Fairness
Act and the FTC's 2024 Trade Regulation Rule on fake/incentivized reviews
(16 C.F.R. Part 465), both of which apply regardless of Florida venue.

## 4. Content standards

Reviews must be about the service experience. We remove content that is:
unlawful, defamatory per se, doxxing (posting someone's private contact
info), hate speech, spam/ads, or unrelated to the job. We do **not** remove
a review merely because it is negative.

## 5. Dispute process (Pros)

1. A Pro may flag a review from their dashboard (or email
   admin@bossofclean.com) within **30 days** of the review being posted,
   stating the specific ground (no underlying job, wrong business, content
   standard violation, suspected fake).
2. Boss of Clean reviews the booking record, messages, and payment history.
3. **SLA:** decision within **10 business days.** Outcomes: keep, remove, or
   annotate. The customer may be invited to revise factual errors.
4. One escalation is available to a second reviewer; that outcome is final
   at the platform level.

## 6. No retaliation

Pros may not threaten, penalize, or refuse warranty service to a customer
because of an honest review. Verified retaliation is grounds for suspension.

## 7. Our role

Boss of Clean is a marketplace. Reviews are the opinions of their authors.
We moderate against this policy but do not endorse review content.
